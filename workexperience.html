My workexperience

As you know who I am,I started working when I was 17 years old. I had a job at a local coffee shop as a barista. Anyone who wants to work in a coffee establishment needs a barista certification. Beans House Coffee is the name of the cafe where I worked. I even received barista instruction there. The owner hired me as a new employee of his store after my training was complete. I made the most of every opportunity to improve my coffee-making abilities and attempted to offer good customer service.During my shift,my duties included:

  • Making and offering a variety of coffee and tea beverages, such as espresso drinks, lattee drinks, iced drinks etc while maintaining the shop's standards of quality and hygiene.
  • Keeping the workplace neat, orderly, and hygienic and ensuring that all the equipment is correctly maintained and sanitized.
  • Ensuring that all of the required ingredients are present in sufficient quantities.
  • Interacting with customers in a pleasant and respectful way.
  • Maintaining a correct cash register and efficiently handling transactions.
  • Maintaining accurate records of cash transactions.

During my tenure at Beans House Cafe, the management team recognized me for my dedication to my duties.

Overall, working as a barista has taught me the value of paying attention to detail, providing good customer service, and having the flexibility to work in a changing environment.
