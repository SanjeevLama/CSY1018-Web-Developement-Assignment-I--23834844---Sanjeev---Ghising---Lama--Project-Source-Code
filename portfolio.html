My Hobbies

Travelling

Since childhood, I've been a fan of nature. I always admire its beauty, so it's natural for me to enjoy travelling to new places and experiencing the beauty of nature.Traveling always rejuvenate my mind and body in a way that nothing else can-- it's a passion of mine that dates back 10 years!

Watching

I've always been fascinated by the magical powers of stories. Hearing stories from elders has made me appreciate its creativity. This love led me to movies, series, animation and other forms of storytelling.Watching movies, series, and animation works is one of my hobbies when I can't travel. I love the suspense and change in plot found in these stories.

Reading

I peruse books as a hobby in my spare time.I peruse a variety of genres in korean manhwa and japanese manga. Typically, a story is showned using a combination of dialogue and images. The stories are vivid and have unique artwork that is visually stunning and have elaborated backgrounds and settings, which aid in developing a feeling of atmosphere and setting.
