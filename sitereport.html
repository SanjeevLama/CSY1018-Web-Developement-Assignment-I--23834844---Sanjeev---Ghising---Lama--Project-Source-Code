Development of site

After receiving a university assignment and following its directions, a portfolio website that displays my coding abilities and personal data was created. Now let's discuss the user experience of the website:

  • Background:The backdrop of my website is a lovely combination of purple and the color of the setting sun. I chose this backdrop because purple is associated with royalty and the color of the setting sun connotes a warm, cozy, creative, and energizing feeling. Clear headings and easy-to-read paragraphs are used in the style, which is well-organized.
  • Navigation menu:The upper right corner of the website is where you can find my navigation menu. My website has a simple, well-labeled navigation menu with distinct categories. Additionally, it has a hover effect to alert users to their presence.
  • Font-family:In my website's headings for the various subjects, I used the arial rounded mt font family. In terms of playfulness, creativity, dependability, and trustworthiness, this typeface family conveys these qualities. The curves and rounded edges of this typeface are well-known. The font's design makes it simple to comprehend.In the body of topics with lengthy paragraphs, I've chosen serif font because it's simpler to read and has a formal, classic appearance.
  • Font-color:I chose the color black for the text because it best complemented the backdrop and exemplified simplicity and elegance.
  • Animation:To grab the viewer's focus, the name on the home page uses animation.
  • Layout:To make the content stand out, box shadows are used. The page's center has been chosen as the alignment for all content.
